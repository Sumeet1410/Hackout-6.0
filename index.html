How It Works

Step 1: Sign Up for Our Service

Register online and choose a subscription plan that suits your farm's size and needs. Once enrolled, you'll have access to our monthly drone checkups and expert analysis.

Step 2: Scheduled Drone Visits

Our team will coordinate regular drone visits to your farm. These visits are scheduled based on your crop cycles and local weather conditions to ensure optimal data collection.

Step 3: High-Resolution Data Collection

Our drones fly over your fields, capturing high-resolution images and detailed sensor data. This aerial survey covers every inch of your crops, identifying areas of concern that may not be visible from the ground.

Step 4: Advanced Analysis and Reporting

The collected data is processed using advanced algorithms and AI to detect signs of pests, diseases, and other potential issues. You'll receive a comprehensive report with actionable insights, including visual maps and detailed findings.

Step 5: Tailored Pesticide Recommendations

Based on the analysis, we provide you with precise pesticide recommendations. These are customized to address the specific issues identified in your crops, ensuring effective and efficient treatments.

Step 6: Apply Solutions and Monitor Progress

With our recommendations in hand, you can apply the necessary pesticides and monitor your crops' health. Our ongoing monthly checkups help you track progress and make adjustments as needed to protect your harvest.
